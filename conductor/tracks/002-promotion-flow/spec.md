# TRACK: 002-promotion-flow

## Specification
Implement a formal Promotion Case workflow in the ECDF application. This workflow allows engineers to propose their promotion to a higher grade based on their existing evidence and check-ins. It requires structured feedback from peers and higher-ranking engineers, as well as final validation by an Independent Technical Assessor (ITA).

## Requirements
- Initiation: Engineers initiate a "Promotion Case" by aggregating evidence and providing a narrative proposition.
- Feedback: Structured feedback statements from endorsers (at least one higher rank) are mandatory.
- Validation: ITA must review the case and validate the proposed scores against the ECDF framework standards.
- Outcome: Final summary showing validated scores, positive points, and development recommendations.
