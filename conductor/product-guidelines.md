# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style
- **Formal & Objective**: Maintain a professional, formal, and objective tone in all application text and documentation.
- **Supportive & Encouraging**: Adopt a supportive, encouraging, and constructive tone for user interactions, especially in feedback and development guidance.
- **Clear & Concise**: Use clear, concise language, avoiding jargon where possible to ensure broad understanding.

## Branding
- **Professional & Reliable**: Emphasize professionalism and reliability in all visual and textual elements, reflecting the serious nature of career development.
- **Modern & Intuitive UX**: Highlight a modern, clean, and intuitive user experience through design and interaction patterns.
- **ECDF-Centric Identity**: Focus on the Engineer Career Development Framework as the core value proposition and central element of the application's identity.

## UX Principles
- **User-Friendly**: Prioritize ease of use and a low learning curve for all users, from engineers to management.
- **Consistent Experience**: Ensure consistency in navigation, layout, and component design across the entire application.
- **Clear Feedback & Guidance**: Provide clear and immediate feedback for user actions and offer proactive guidance to help users understand their progress and next steps.
