# Product Guidelines

## Aesthetic
- **Premium Dark Mode**: The application must utilize a dark mode aesthetic featuring glassmorphism elements and modern typography. Styling must be done with Vanilla CSS (no Tailwind).

## Form Defaults
- **Blank Placeholders**: Form `<select>` elements used for scoring (e.g., the 1-5 Dreyfus scale) must always default to a blank, disabled `<option>` placeholder to ensure intentional and deliberate scoring by both employees and assessors.

## Dashboard View
- **KPI Centric**: The primary focus of the employee dashboard should be on current pillar scores and metrics, giving users a clear view of their standing against expected targets. *(Note: As per existing project constraints, a list of historical check-in records must also be rendered on the dashboard).*

## Tone and Prose
- **Conversational & Coaching**: The language used throughout the application should be friendly and encouraging. It should promote a culture of continuous feedback, helping employees feel supported in their career development journey.
