# Product Definition

## Initial Concept
The ECDF application is a performance and career development platform designed to help engineers track their growth against the Engineer Career Development Framework.

## Target Users
- **Internal Engineers**: For performance and career tracking within a large organization.
- **HR & Management**: For oversight of engineering talent development and performance management.
- **Individual Contributors**: For self-improvement and guided career progression.

## Main Goals
- **Career Advancement**: Provide a clear, structured path for engineers to advance their careers within the organization.
- **Performance Evaluation**: Enable fair, transparent, and evidence-based performance evaluations.
- **Automated Personal Development Plans (PDPs)**: Streamline and automate the creation and tracking of personal development plans.

## Key Features
- **Evidence-based Assessments**: Facilitate assessments against the Engineer Career Development Framework, supported by collected evidence.
- **Continuous Feedback Check-ins**: Allow for regular, point-in-time check-ins to provide continuous feedback and track progress.
- **Automated PDP Generation**: Automatically generate personalized development plans based on assessment results and framework alignment.
